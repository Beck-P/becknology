# Becknology — Ideas

## Apps

- **Conway's Game of Life** — Interactive cellular automata in the purple/black palette. Click to place cells, watch them evolve. Classic hacker toy.
- **Generative art** — Algorithmic visuals that create a unique piece each load. Particle systems, flow fields, or geometric patterns. Screenshot-worthy.
- **Reaction time tester** — Wait for the screen to change, click as fast as you can. Shows time in ms. Simple, addictive, competitive.
- **ASCII art converter** — Upload or paste an image, get ASCII art output. Perfectly on-brand.

## Site-wide polish

- **Custom 404 page** — ASCII art "404", glitch animation, "you've wandered into the void" message. Everyone hits 404s eventually.
- **Favicon** — Pixel-art "B" in purple. First thing people see in the browser tab.
- **OG meta tags / social card** — Dark card with "BECKNOLOGY" that renders when someone shares a URL in Slack/iMessage/Twitter.
- **Page transition effects** — Glitch/dissolve animation when clicking hub cards before navigating. Makes the site feel like one cohesive retro OS.
- **Mouse cursor trail** — Subtle pixelated purple dots following the cursor on desktop, fading out. Makes moving the mouse feel fun.
- **View source easter egg** — Big ASCII art comment at the top of each HTML file rewarding people who inspect the code.
